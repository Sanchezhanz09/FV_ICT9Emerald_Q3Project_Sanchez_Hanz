AYRTON SENNA
About Personal Life Racing Career
Ayrton Senna da Silva was a Brazillian Formula 1 racing driver from 1984 to 1994. He won the 1988, 1990, and 1991 Formula 1 World Driver's Championship while driving for the McLaren Racing Team. He is well known for his excellent driving skills and gained a reputation for being a fierce competitor on track. Ayrton Senna was a devouted Catholic and had a profound faith to God. He also has an organization that helps underpriviledged kids in Brazil. He donated millions of his money to educate Brazil's children. Ayrton Senna had a very good career during his time. He won 3 World Driver's Championships, 41 races, 80 podiums, and 65 pole positions. However, we never got to see his full potentiel. On May 1, 1994 Senna died due to a mechanical failure of his car that caused it to lose control and crashed on the barrier. Experts state when Senna's car hit the barrier it was travelling over 233 km/h (145 mph).
